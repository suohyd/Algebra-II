Definition 9.6.1. (Sum of submodules).

The sum of \(R\)-submodules \(M_i\) is the submodule generated by \(\bigcup_{i\in I}M_i\text{.}\) It is denoted by \(\sum_{i\in I}M_i\text{.}\) Thus
\begin{equation*} \sum_{i\in I}M_i=\left\{\sum_im_i:m_i\in M_i\;\text{are such that all but finitely many}\;m_i\;\text{are zero}\right\} \end{equation*}
