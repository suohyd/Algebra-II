Item 2.

For any \(r,s\in R\) we have
\begin{equation*} 0\cdot r=r\cdot 0=0\quad\text{and}\quad (-r)\cdot s=-(rs)\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(-r\) and \(-(rs)\) are the additive inverses of \(r\) and \(rs\text{,}\) respectively. In particular, the additive inverse of \(r\in R\) is \(-1\cdot r\text{.}\)
