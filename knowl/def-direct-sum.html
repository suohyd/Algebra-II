Definition 9.6.4. (Direct sum of modules).

Consider a family \((N_i)_{i\in I}\) of left modules over a ring \(R\text{.}\) The direct sum of \((N_i)_{i\in I}\) is the following submodule of \(\prod_{i\in I}N_i\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \bigoplus_{k\in I}N_k=\left\{(x_k)_{k\in I}\in\prod_{k\in I}N_k:\pi_i((x_k)_{k\in I})=0\;\text{except for a finite number of indices}\right\} \end{equation*}
