Theorem 4.10.1. (Chinese remainder theorem).

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and let \(\mfa_1,\mfa_2,\ldots,\mfa_n\) be proper ideals of \(A\text{.}\) Further assume that for \(i\neq j\text{,}\) \(\mfa_i+\mfa_j=A\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} A\big/\mfa_1\cdots\mfa_n\simeq A\big/\mfa_1\cap\cdots\cap\mfa_n\simeq A/\mfa_1\times\cdots\times A/\mfa_n. \end{equation*}
