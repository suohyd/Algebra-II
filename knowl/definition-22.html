Definition 1.5.12. (Subalgebra generated by a subset).

Let \(A\) be an \(F\)-algebra and \(S\) be a nonempty subset of \(A\text{.}\) The subalgebra generated by \(S\) is the intersection of all subalgebras of \(A\) containing \(S\text{.}\) It is denoted by \(F[S]\text{.}\) Thus,
\begin{align*} F[S]\amp=\bigg\{a_0\cdot 1+\sum a_{i_1\cdots i_r}\cdot s_{i_1}s_{i_2}\cdots s_{i_r}:\;i_j,r\in\N\text{ and }s_{i_j}\in S\\ \amp\quad\qquad\qquad a_0,a_{i_1\cdots i_r}\in F\text{ and all but finitely many }a_{i_1\ldots i_r}= 0\bigg\} \end{align*}
