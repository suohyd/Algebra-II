Definition 9.6.3. (Projection maps).

Let \((N_i)_{i\in I}\) be a family of left modules over a ring \(R\text{.}\) For each \(i\) we get the family of \(R\)-module homomorphism
\begin{equation*} \pi_i\colon\prod_{k\in I}N_k\rightarrow N_i\quad\text{given by}\;(x_k)_{k\in I}\mapsto x_i. \end{equation*}
An \(R\)-mod homomorphism \(\pi_i\) is called the projection homomorphism.
