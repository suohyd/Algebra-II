Let \(V\) be a vector space over a field \(F\text{.}\) Consider the set of all endomorphisms of \(V\text{,}\) \(\End_F{V}\) i.e., the set of all \(F\)-linear maps from \(V\) to \(V\text{.}\) This is a ring under following operations.
  1. Addition: \((f+g)(v)=\underbrace{f(v)+g(v)}_{\text{addition in }V}\) for \(f,g\in\End_F(V)\) and \(v\in V\)
  2. Multiplication: \((f\circ g)(v)=f\left(g(v)\right)\) for \(f,g\in\End_F(V)\) and \(v\in V\)
