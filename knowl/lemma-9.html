Lemma 4.3.1.

Let \(R\) be a ring and let \(f,g\in R[X]\text{.}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \deg(f+g)\leq{\rm max}{\left\{\deg(f),\deg(g)\right\}}\)
  2. \(\deg(fg)\leq\deg(f)+\deg(g)\) with equality if and only if
    \begin{equation*} LC(fg)=LC(f)\;LC(g)\quad\text{is nonzero}\quad. \end{equation*}
