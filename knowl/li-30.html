Item 1. [Smallest left ideal containing both \(\mfa\) and \(\mfb\)].

\(\mathfrak{a}+\mathfrak{b}=\{a+b:a\in\mathfrak{a}\text{ and }b\in\mathfrak{b}\}\text{.}\) More generally if \(\mfa_i\) are left ideals of \(R\) then
\begin{equation*} \sum\mfa_i=\left\{\sum_ix_i:x_i\in\mfa_i\text{ and all but finitely many}\quad x_i=0\right\}. \end{equation*}
