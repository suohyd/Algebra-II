Item 5.

If \(\mfa,\mfb\) and \(\mathfrak{c}\) are ideals (i.e., two-sided ideals) in \(R\) then we have following identities.
\begin{align*} \mfa(\mfb+\mfc)\amp=\mfa\mfb+\mfa\mfc\\ (\mfa+\mfb)\mfc\amp=\mfa\mfc+\mfb\mfc\\ \text{If}\;\mfb\subseteq\mfa\;\text{or}\;\mfc\subseteq\mfa\quad\text{then}\quad\amp\mfa\cap(\mfb+\mfc)=(\mfa\cap\mfb)+(\mfa\cap\mfc) \end{align*}
